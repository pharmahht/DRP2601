# Pharmacist Clinical Training Simulator 💊

## Case Study: Drug-related gingival enlargement and safe medication counseling
## This learning material was designed by: Professor. 陳崇鈺 Research Team (Huu-Hiep Tran)
### 📋 Overview
This interactive web-based simulator is designed for pharmacy students to practice identifying **Drug-Related Problems (DRPs)** and providing patient counselling. 

The case focuses on:
- **Ms. Nguyen (56yo):** Presenting with drug-induced gingival overgrowth.
- **Clinical Challenge:** Identifying **Amlodipine** as the causative agent and managing the safety of **Alendronate** in a patient with a history of GERD and esophagitis.

### 🎯 Learning Objectives
After completing this simulator, students will be able to:
1. Identify common medications associated with gingival enlargement (e.g., Calcium Channel Blockers).
2. Provide correct administration counseling for oral bisphosphonates to minimize esophageal injury.
3. Propose alternative therapies (e.g., non-oral options) when oral administration is unsafe or not feasible.
4. Practice collaborative communication between pharmacists, dentists, and physicians.
